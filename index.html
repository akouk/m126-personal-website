    I'm an experienced engineer passionate about the intersection of technology and innovation. With a background in surveying and geoinformatics, I've honed my skills in fields like photogrammetry, computer vision, augmented and virtual reality.

    I spent several years at up2metric P.C., where I delved into researching state-of-the-art algorithms and software libraries for 3D reconstruction technologies. My work focused on developing workflows for creating photorealistic 3D reconstructions tailored for augmented and virtual reality environments.

    My educational journey includes pursuing a Master's degree in Information and Communication Technologies from the National & Kapodistrian University of Athens and holding a Bachelor's degree in Surveying & Geoinformatics Engineering from the University of West Attica.



    Visualization

    Same Sex Marriage Laws in the USA | 1995-2015



    This is an animated violin plot, tracking the evolution of same-sex marriage laws for two decades (1995-2015) providing valuable historical context. It allows viewers to see the progression of LGBTQ+ rights in the United States, which is both informative and emotionally impactful.

    The visualization caught my eye and held my attention, due to its colors, design, and animation style. Moreover, the visualization presents the data in a clear and easy-to-understand manner, making it accessible to a wide audience.
